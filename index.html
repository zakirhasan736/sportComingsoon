WHO WE ARE?

LEGENDSLEAD is an early stage sports industry professional network and marketplace. We are currently preparing to launch an online teaching and learning platform. Our instructors will include notable professionals in every sports discipline, top-flight academic leaders, corporate and media heavyweights, active and retired athletes, and certifiable experts in the macro and micro disciplines of the sports business. We aim to become the leading provider of high-quality live and on-demand educational content focused on the sports industry.

Our platform will help students at the grassroots level, young to seasoned professionals, job seekers, athletes, and enthusiasts to gain new skills, find new jobs, mentorships, and advance their careers by learning from the best people in the sports industry. Essentially, our offerings are uniquely designed to serve our entire network “from cradle to grave” MEANING users will have something for the entire lifecycle of their career.
